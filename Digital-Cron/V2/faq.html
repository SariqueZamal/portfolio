Frequently Asked Questions

Common Queries & Insights

Find comprehensive details regarding our high-performance web development pipelines, AI integration methods, security practices, and project timelines.

What services does Digital Cron specialize in?
We specialize in two core pillars: architecting high-performance digital storefronts (web development) and deploying autonomous AI systems (voice agents, intelligent chatbots, CRM automations, and workflow integrations).
What technologies do you use to ensure high-performance, fast-loading websites?
We leverage modern frameworks, JAMstack, and highly optimized layouts (including Vite, Next.js, and custom static generators). By compressing media, utilizing global CDNs, and prioritizing lean HTML/CSS execution, we build digital storefronts that load in sub-seconds and convert leads on contact.
How do your custom AI agents and chatbots integrate with our existing CRM?
Our builds are fully compatible with mainstream CRM platforms like HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, ActiveCampaign, and other REST-API based databases. We construct secure webhooks that automatically send parsed customer answers, leads, calendar bookings, and contextual details to your pipelines in real-time.
What does the project timeline look like from onboarding to launch?
Project cycles are custom-scoped. High-performance website architecture builds typically range from 4 to 6 weeks. Deeper autonomous integrations, such as custom LLM training, CRM sync maps, or AI voice systems, average 6 to 8 weeks, including pipeline audits and system debugging.
How do you handle security and data protection for AI systems?
Security is embedded in our engineering. We establish closed API pipelines, use AES-256 data encryption for resting storage, deploy SSL/TLS protocols for transit routes, and enforce multi-factor validation credentials (OAuth) to make sure your proprietary operational data remains strictly secure.
What is your pricing model? Do you offer retainer-based maintenance?
We leverage flat-rate project pricing for custom builds and system integrations. Post-launch, we provide flexible monthly optimization retainers. These retainers cover continuous model calibration, code updates, security patching, prompt refinements, and general technical support.
Can your AI voice agents handle complex customer support and booking flows?
Yes. Our AI voice engines integrate contextual memory structures and dynamic functions. They can naturally book meetings, route dispatch workflows, answer domain-specific questions from training documentation, and push transactional results to databases during standard calls.
Will we receive support and training after the systems are deployed?
Yes, absolutely. We hand over recorded training videos, prompt guidelines, API keys, and workflow diagrams. We also offer standard support periods after launch to ensure your systems remain stable, and prompt adjustments can be deployed smoothly as operations grow.
